THEORY:  
 
An Armstrong  number  (also  called  a narcissistic  number)  is a number  that is equal  to the 
sum of the cubes of its digits. For a three -digit number abc, it is an Armstrong number if: 
a³ + b³ + c³ = abc  
For example,  371 is an Armstrong  number  because:  
3³ + 7³ + 1³ = 27 + 343 + 1 = 371.
